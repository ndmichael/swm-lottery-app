PROFILE

Can I change my username?

No, usernames can only be entered once during creation of account and cannot be editable.

How can I change my password?

Go to the profile page by clicking on the icon on the menu bar, there you'd find a form to enter old password and new password. Fill it and click "Submit".

What if I forget my password?

In the situation where you've forgotten your password, you can always reset your password in the Log In page by clicking "Forgot Password", after which a password reset mail will be sent to your mail box.

GAME

How do I play?

To play, every user is expected to first make a deposit to fund their wallet, after which they can then head out to the game section and click games, then select their choice of game and click play.

How soon after the draws will the results be out?

Results for each draws are immediate.

How do I check my ticket?

Go to profile section on the menu bar, select profile then click on "Tickets".

Do tickets expire?

No, tickets don't expire.

What is the minimum and maximum amount I can play on the game?

The minimum amount a user can play with is N100 and maximum any amount.

How much can I withdraw at once?

You can withdraw any amount of you choice, as long as you have that amount of money in your wallet.

Will there be charges for withdrawals?

SWM Lottery does'nt charge any fee for withdrawals or deposits made.
